The Intelligence Behind the Price

AutoValuate isn't just a basic calculator. It's powered by an advanced Gradient Boosting Machine Learning Model trained on over 8,000 real-world used car transactions from the Indian market.

1. Data Processing

We normalize inputs taking into account engine CC, max power, mileage (kmpl), transmission type, and fuel type. Each feature is mathematically scaled to understand its true weight in the market.

2. Predictive Modelling

Our XGBoost algorithm evaluates non-linear relationships—like how a luxury brand depreciates faster initially, but holds steady value after 5 years compared to budget brands.

3. Market Context

We penalize multiple ownerships and high kilometer usage exactly as the market does, giving you a realistic selling or buying target instead of an optimistic guess.

4. Deal Verdicts

By comparing the AI's fair value against a seller's asking price, we instantly tag listings as a Great Deal, Fair Price, or Overpriced to protect buyers from overpaying.
